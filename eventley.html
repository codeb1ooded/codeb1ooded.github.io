Eventley

To analyze popularity of events near you

About

Whenever, you want to utilize your weekend in some great and enjoying holiday, you would most probably found yourselves confused of where to spend that time to make most of it. Or when you visit a new place and have no idea of main attractions of place, then what to do? That's where, Eventley came to rescue. Eventley help you to find popular and relevant(weather on day is fine) events near you on basis of your choice of category (like education, sports, culture, entertainment, etc.) by analyzing events based on number of likes, number of comments, number of images , weather at time of event, etc.

Beautiful frontend with geolocation to help locate you

Select category and area to be covered

Data collection in back-end

Attractive Visualizations to help you decide where to go next

Technologies Used

Frontend Development: HTML, CSS, JS
Backend Development: Python and Django
APIs: Eventful api, Google Maps, Open whether api, Plotly for visualization
