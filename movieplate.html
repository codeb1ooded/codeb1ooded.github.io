MoviePlate

An android app to let you browse a movie or TV show for you

About

Get bored? Want to watch a movie or TV Show? It is an android app to let you find a movie or TV show for you by letting you browse popular, rated and now playing movies and TV shows. Also it allows you to add and remove TV shows or movies from your watchlist and see your watchlist.

Developers

Himanshu Singhal, Manisha Poddar, Megha Aggarwal, Sachin Aggarwal

View code on github

Technologies Used

Development: XML, Java, Android support
Android Libraries: Appcompat, design, Retrofit, GSON, Picasso library
API's: Moviedb, OMDB
